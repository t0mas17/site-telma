Descobre o Teu Nível de Inglês

Responde a 5 perguntas rápidas e descobre o teu nível atual. É grátis e demora menos de 2 minutos!

1. Complete: "She ___ to the gym every day."

go
goes
going
goed

2. Choose the correct sentence:

I have been to Paris last year.
I went to Paris last year.
I was go to Paris last year.
I did went to Paris last year.

3. "If I ___ rich, I would travel the world."

am
will be
were
would be

4. What does "Nevertheless" mean?

Because of this
In addition
In spite of that
As a result

5. "The report ___ by the time the meeting started."

was finished
had been finished
has been finished
finished

🎉 O teu nível é:

B1 - Intermédio
